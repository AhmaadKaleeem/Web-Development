Directorate of Students Affairs

The Student Affairs Department at Air University, led by the Director of Student Affairs, works collaboratively with a Proctor, Assistant Proctor (female), and an Office Assistant. This dedicated team ensures the implementation of the university's Code of Conduct, which includes maintaining the dress code, discipline, and behavioral standards.
