Recent Events

Air Nexus

Air Nexus is one of the most anticipated events at Air University. It brings together students from various disciplines to showcase their talents and compete in various competitions. The event includes tech exhibitions, cultural performances, and much more.

Welcome Party

The Welcome Party is an annual event organized to welcome new students to Air University. It is a fun-filled evening with music, dance, and various performances by students. It provides a great opportunity for new students to interact and make new friends.

Air University Departments

Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science at Air University offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers various aspects of computer science, including programming, data structures, algorithms, and software engineering. The department is equipped with state-of-the-art labs and facilities to provide students with hands-on experience.

Department of Electrical Engineering

The Department of Electrical Engineering at Air University provides students with a strong foundation in electrical engineering principles and practices. The curriculum includes courses in circuit analysis, electronics, power systems, and control systems. The department also offers research opportunities in various areas of electrical engineering.
