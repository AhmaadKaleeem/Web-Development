Student Affairs Department: Supporting Student Growth and Development

The Student Affairs Department at Air University, led by the Director of Student Affairs, works collaboratively with a Proctor, Assistant Proctor (female), and an Office Assistant. This dedicated team ensures the implementation of the university's Code of Conduct, which includes maintaining the dress code, discipline, and behavioral standards.

Activities and Events

The department plays a pivotal role in fostering a vibrant campus life by facilitating and organizing various inter, intra, and extracurricular activities. These include:

  • Welcome and Farewell Parties
  • Student Week
  • Declamation Contests
  • Funfairs
  • Seminars
  • Variety Programs
  • National Day Celebrations
  • Educational and Recreational Visits

Event Management

To streamline event organization, the department processes requests submitted through the Event Management Form, accessible on the Air University website. The team provides comprehensive support, including financial, administrative, and logistical guidance, ensuring the success of indoor and outdoor activities conducted by students, departments, societies, and clubs.

Student Support

In addition to event facilitation, the Student Affairs Department actively assists in resolving student concerns through counseling and corrective measures. When necessary, they engage with parents to address specific issues and offer valuable guidance to students, promoting their personal and academic growth.
